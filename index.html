Our Story

Meet The Owner

Hi! I’m the owner and creator behind Yuumii — a small business built with passion, creativity, and love for baking.

What started as a simple hobby quickly became something bigger when I discovered how much joy customized cakes and cupcakes can bring to every celebration. From birthdays and weddings to simple sweet cravings, I love creating desserts that not only taste delicious but also make special moments even more memorable.

Every cake and cupcake is carefully designed with attention to detail, using quality ingredients and creative ideas to match each customer’s vision. I enjoy turning themes, colors, and inspirations into edible art that people can truly enjoy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering your favorite treats.

Simply click any "Order Now" or "Inquiry" button to open our order form. Once submitted, your order details are copied, and you'll be redirected to our Facebook Messenger. Just paste the details and send! We'll confirm your order and send payment instructions there.

We accept payments via GCash, Maya, and Bank Transfers (BDO/BPI). We require a 50% downpayment to secure your slot, and the balance is settled upon delivery or pick-up.

Yes! We offer delivery within Metro Manila and nearby provinces. Delivery fees depend on your location and are handled via third-party couriers (Lalamove/Grab). For tiered cakes, we recommend GrabCar to ensure safe transport.

For custom cakes, we recommend ordering at least 5-7 days in advance. For Kakanin bilao, 2-3 days is usually sufficient. We do occasionally have slots for rush orders, so feel free to message us!
